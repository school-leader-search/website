FAQ

Is this a recruitment agency?

No. School Leader Search is a profiles database and invitation system. We do not place candidates or take commissions. Schools invite leaders to visit and proceed with their own recruitment processes.

Do you perform DBS checks?

We prompt identity verification and references when profiles are created. Schools must complete all statutory checks (DBS, references, right to work) before any employment.

Which phases and regions are included?

Primary, Secondary and Special settings across England initially, with plans to expand across the UK. Filter by region and phase when browsing.

How are profiles written?

From a structured 20-minute call. We focus on impact, strengths, context and preferences. Leaders review and approve before anything is visible.

What about confidentiality?

Leaders control visibility and can pause or withdraw at any time. Schools only see approved profiles.
